Health Benefits of Dates

Dates types

Explore The Categories Of dates

Sokari

Dates Sukkari Rutab is distinguished by its large grain and golden outer crust. It was called sugary due to its sweetness and taste. One of the most important characteristics of Dates Sukkari Rutab is that it has a caramel-like taste.

Rothana

Rothana is a type of wet dates that is famous for Madinah Al-Munawwarah in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. It ripens in mid-June of each year and its season ends quickly. It has an oval shape, and the color is yellow and the wet is half brown and half yellow.

Ajwah

ajwa date is a celebrated assortment of dates, grown exclusively in Medina, Saudi Arabia. Ranging from a dark brown to almost black colour, these dates are delightfully soft and fruity with a prune-like taste and tend to be of a dry variety
